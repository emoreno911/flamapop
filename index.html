What is Notitube?

Notitube is a Google Chrome extension that will allow you to have desktop notifications every time a Youtube video changes in your browser, you can skip videos directly from your notifications or even make likes if you want It.

With this extension you also will be able to control the reproduction status of the current video from any tab in your browser; pause, play or skip to the next video at any moment without switch from your current tab, It's a perfect fit for the Youtube's playlist.

Features
  • Desktop notifications
  • Play, pause or skip your videos from any tab
  • Loop button to repeat the current video
