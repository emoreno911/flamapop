What is Flamapop?

Flamapop is a Google Chrome extension that allows to control the playback of the current Youtube video from any tab in your browser; pause, play or skip to the next video at any moment without switching from your current tab, It's a perfect fit for your Youtube playlists.

With Flamapop you will also be able to have desktop notifications every time a Youtube video changes in your browser, you can skip videos directly from your notifications or even like videos if you want It.

Features
  • Play, pause or skip your videos from any tab
  • Loop button to repeat the current video
  • Desktop notifications
Controls Reference
  • Play/Pause Buttons Sets the current video on play or pause
  • Skip Button Skip to the next video on the playlist
  • Toggle Loop On enabled It sets the current video to be repeated
  • Toggle Notifications Enable or disable desktop notifications
  • Toggle Skip Enable or disable the Skip option on desktop notifications
  • Toggle Likes Enable or disable the Like option on desktop notifications
